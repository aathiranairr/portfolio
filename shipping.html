Shipping & Delivery Options

We offer reliable shipping options across India to ensure your journaling supplies reach you safely.

Free Shipping

We are happy to offer FREE Shipping on all orders above ₹1,499.

Shipping Method

Standard Delivery: 4–9 Business Days

In certain situations, we may partner with trusted third-party logistics providers to fulfill and deliver your order.

Orders are usually dispatched within 2–3 business days after order confirmation. During festivals, sale periods, or unforeseen circumstances, dispatch may take slightly longer.

Delivery Timeline:

  • Bangalore: Usually 2–5 days after dispatch.
  • Other States: Usually 5–11 days after dispatch.

Delivery may be delayed due to weather conditions, natural calamities, public holidays, courier issues, or other unavoidable circumstances.

Incorrect Shipping Information

If a parcel is returned because of an incorrect address, incorrect phone number, or if the customer is unavailable to receive the shipment, the customer will be responsible for both the return shipping charges and the re-shipping charges.

Delivered Orders

If a prepaid order is marked as delivered but you believe you have not received it, please contact us within 48 hours of the delivery date. Claims made after this period may not be eligible for investigation.

Delivery Coverage

Tara Notes currently ships only within India.

If your PIN code is not serviceable by our courier partners, we will dispatch your order through India Post Speed Post. Speed Post shipments are generally dispatched every Monday or Tuesday.

Delayed Orders

Unexpected delays may occur because of logistics issues, adverse weather, high order volumes, courier delays, or other unforeseen events.

If your shipment is delayed, we will keep you informed and provide updated delivery estimates whenever possible.

Returns & Exchanges

For information regarding refunds, returns, or exchanges, please refer to our Return & Refund Policy.
